Banana Pancakes

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making soft and fluffy banana pancakes! These simple pancakes are perfect for breakfast or a quick snack. Follow this easy recipe to create delicious pancakes that are lightly sweet and full of banana flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, mash the banana and whisk it with the egg and milk.
  4. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet mixture and stir until smooth.
  5. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown, then serve warm.
